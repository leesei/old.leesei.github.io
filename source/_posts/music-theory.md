where:

- $L$ is the length of string
- $T$ is tension of string
- $\rho$ is density of string

- multiply by fifths (\* 3/2)
- lower by an octave is if it is highter than one (\* 1/2)
- requires the Pythagoras comma for correction every 12 notes (7 octives)
